Meet Stressless.

Stressless is a platform for employers to manage office stress levels. Using the heart-rate measurements from wearables worn by your team, we extract heart rate variability statistics that have been shown to be related to stress. We give you these metrics to schedule your meetings at optimal times, provide recommendations and effectively test new wellness programs.

Capture Stress.

Stressless uses sophisticated signal-processing to convert simple heart rates into meaningful metrics that you can use to measure the wellbeing of your team.

Visualize Stress Throughout the Day.

Stressless breaks down the day into hourly segments to calculate hourly stress levels. These indicators can be used to see which times of the day your team finds the most stressful.

Recommendations.

Stressless helps you become a better manager by giving you tips and tricks to improve relations with your team and suggests strategies to increase their health and wellbeing.

History.

Stressless lets you move back in the past and track the evolution of stress in time. If you are doing a good job, this graph should be decreasing!

Smart Recommendations. Coming Soon

Upon reaching a critical amount of data, Stressless will provide you with smarter and more team-oriented recommendations based on how your teams' stress patterns evolve. This will allow you to get specific time-periods where your team might have collectively high levels of stress.

Rest Indicators. Coming Soon

Wearables collect more than just heart rates, Stressless is able to incorporate multiple signals including restfulness to determine how to kick off your day.
