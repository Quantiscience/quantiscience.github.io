About Us

The number of work-related health issues are growing with the increasing demands of the economy. Anxiety, depression, stress and high blood pressure are only a few of the major effects that can be caused by the workplace. Companies invest in benefits and the work environment to ensure their emplpoyees can maintain a work-life balance. However, the issues haven't gone away. Our product, Stressless monitors physiological signals of employees during the day to recommend how to improve the work-life balance and reduce work-related health issues.
